GreenRoots Community Garden

Growing Together, Sustaining the Future

Site Name

GreenRoots Community Garden - This name represents our local urban gardening initiative focused on sustainability, community building, and healthy living. It reflects our mission to grow food, foster community relationships, and encourage environmental stewardship.

Optional domain availability: greenrootsgarden.org

Site Purpose

The GreenRoots Community Garden website will serve as an informational and interactive hub for our local gardening initiative. It will provide an overview of our mission, offer volunteer and donation opportunities, showcase upcoming events, display a photo gallery of our progress, and provide an easy way to get in touch. Interactive features will include an FAQ accordion, event countdown timer, and a garden tip-of-the-day generator.

Scenarios

  • How can I volunteer at the community garden?
  • When is the next planting day or gardening workshop?
  • How can I donate to support the garden’s activities?

Color Schema

  • Forest Green (#2E8B57) – For headings, navigation bar, and key highlights.
  • Sunshine Yellow (#FFD700) – For buttons, accents, and call-to-action sections.
  • Light Beige (#FAF3E0) – For background color to keep a natural, warm feel.

Typography

  • Lora (serif) – For headings to convey elegance and nature-inspired tone.
  • Open Sans (sans-serif) – For body text to ensure readability and clarity.
